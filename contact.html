Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I make a reservation?

Simply browse our café listings, select your preferred café, choose your date and time, and confirm your reservation. You'll receive an instant confirmation.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes! You can cancel or modify your reservation up to 2 hours before your scheduled time through your account dashboard or by contacting us directly.

Is there a booking fee?

No, CafeConnect is completely free for customers. We don't charge any booking fees - you only pay for what you order at the café.

How can cafés join CafeConnect?

Café owners can apply to join our platform by filling out our partnership form or contacting us directly. We'll guide you through the onboarding process.
